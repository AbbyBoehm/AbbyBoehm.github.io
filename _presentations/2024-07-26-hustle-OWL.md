I summarized the latest progress of the HUSTLE Treasury Program, including providing updates on the current understanding of the atmosphere of WASP-127 b and the state of HUSTLE-tools, then known as ExoTiC-UVIS. I highlighted progress that had been made specifically at OWL, including the submission of our first paper for review, the creation of new UV-IR cross-sections, and the first commits to the HUSTLE-tools GitHub repository.
