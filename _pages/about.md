Education
------
I joined Cornell University's Astronomy and Space Sciences Program in 2022. I passed my candidacy exam in 2025 and earned an M.S. in Astronomy and Space Sciences with a focus in Exoplanets. My anticipated dissertation defense date is in June of 2027.

I graduated summa cum laude from Penn State University in 2022 with a B.S. in Astrophysics, a B.S. in Physics, and a Minor in Mathematics.
