title: Juniper
---
Juniper is an open-source data reduction pipeline for time-series observations with JWST.

<div style="text-align: justify; text-justify: inter-word;">
  <p><a href="https://github.com/AbbyBoehm/Juniper">Juniper</a> is an open-source modular pipeline for reduction of JWST time-series data, specialized for supporting observations of exoplanets. Juniper wraps Stages 1 and 2 of the official <a href="https://jwst-pipeline.readthedocs.io/en/latest/">jwst</a> pipeline and combines these with custom stages to support reduction, extraction, and fitting 1D spectral time-series of exoplanet transits and eclipses. Following industry standards set by <a href="https://eurekadocs.readthedocs.io/en/latest/">Eureka!</a> and <a href="https://hustle-tools.readthedocs.io/en/main/">HUSTLE-tools</a>, Juniper is likewise designed to be executed easily and reliably through the use of .berry configuration files which record the details of each run and allow users to share their exact processes with their colleagues. Juniper currently supports NIRSpec and MIRI LRS observations, with plans to add support for MIRI photometry and NIRISS SOSS time-series observations in the near future.</p>
  
  <p>Documentation for Juniper is coming soon!</p>
</div>
